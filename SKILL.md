# News Tracer Skill

## Overview

News Tracer helps you verify news authenticity by:
- Tracing the earliest news sources
- Generating complete timelines
- Checking authoritative media coverage
- Identifying risks from exaggerated or unverified news

## When to Use

Use this skill when you need to:
1. Verify if a news story is real or fake
2. Find the original source of a news report
3. Generate a timeline of news development
4. Cross-verify multiple media reports
5. Assess the credibility of news before making trading decisions
